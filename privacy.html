Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

We collect your email address, display name, and player ID upon registration. Location data is used only for finding nearby courts and is not stored permanently. Check-in history and booking records are stored to provide analytics and booking management features.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide core app functionality including court discovery, bookings, check-ins, friend connections, and analytics. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Your account data is retained while your account is active. You may request account deletion by contacting support. Booking records may be retained for venue compliance purposes.
