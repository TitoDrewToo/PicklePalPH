How It Works

Step 1

Download the app, sign up, and log in. Your PPID is shown above your display name — tap to copy it.

Step 2

Prepare the required documents listed below before applying.

Step 3

Submit your email for verification. You’ll receive a secure onboarding form link after confirmation.

Requirements

PicklePal supports both community and commercial venues. Requirements vary slightly depending on your venue type.

Community / Barangay / HOA

  • Valid ID
  • Proof of role (Barangay / HOA / association document)
  • Authorized representative for payouts

School / Institution

  • Employee / admin ID
  • Proof of association with school
  • Authorized payout account

Commercial Venue

  • Business registration (DTI / SEC / permit)
  • Owner or manager verification
  • Business or authorized payout account
